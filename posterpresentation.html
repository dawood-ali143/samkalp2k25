Creative works, historical research, philosophical inquiries, and cultural studies.

Poster Guidelines

  • Posters should be 36" x 48" (width x height) in portrait orientation.
  • Include title, authors, affiliation, introduction, methods, results, conclusion, and references.
  • Use clear, legible fonts (minimum 24pt for body text, 36pt for headings).
  • Incorporate visuals like graphs, charts, and images to enhance understanding.
  • Maintain a balance between text and visuals for optimal readability.
  • Presenters must be available at their posters during the assigned session.
  • Electronic copies of posters must be submitted one week before the event.

For any queries regarding poster specifications or content, please contact the event coordinators.
