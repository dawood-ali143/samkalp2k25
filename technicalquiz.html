Quiz Rules & Guidelines

  • The quiz will consist of multiple rounds including multiple-choice questions, coding problems, and technical puzzles.
  • Participants must register before the deadline to be eligible.
  • All participants must bring their own laptops for the coding rounds.
  • Use of any external resources or communication during the quiz is strictly prohibited.
  • The decision of the quiz masters will be final and binding.
  • Participants should arrive at least 30 minutes before the scheduled start time.

For any queries regarding the rules or registration process, please contact the event coordinators.
