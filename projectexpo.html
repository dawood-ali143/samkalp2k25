Social Impact

Projects addressing social challenges, sustainable solutions, and technology for community development.

Project Guidelines & Requirements

  • Projects must be original work developed by the participating team
  • Each team can have 2-4 members from same or different departments
  • Prepare a 5-minute demonstration and 3-minute Q&A session
  • Bring all necessary equipment, extensions, and backup power sources
  • Display boards (3x4 feet) will be provided for each project
  • Submit project abstract and documentation one week before the event
  • Projects will be evaluated on innovation, implementation, and impact
  • Ensure your project is fully functional during the exhibition hours

For technical requirements and setup assistance, please contact the expo coordinators at least 3 days before the event.
