Paper Guidelines & Submission

  • Papers should be original, unpublished work and follow IEEE format (maximum 6 pages)
  • Include abstract (250 words), introduction, methodology, results, discussion, and references
  • Presentation time: 10 minutes for presentation + 5 minutes for Q&A
  • Submit both PDF and DOC versions of your paper through the online portal
  • Plagiarism should be less than 15% (will be checked through plagiarism detection software)
  • All authors must be present during their scheduled presentation slot
  • Use PowerPoint or PDF format for presentation slides with minimum 24pt font size

For template downloads and submission portal access, please visit our event website or contact the coordinators.
